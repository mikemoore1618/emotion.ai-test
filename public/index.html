Emotion.ai

Emotion AI is a meetup series dedicated to exploring the multidisciplinary intersection of artificial emotional intelligence, neuroscience, art, and more. We'll bring together technical and non-technical speakers and audiences to have talks, conversations, and interactive activities, all looking at and exploring the theme of emotion and feeling from the perspectives of AI (deep learning, machine learning), software, neuroscience, psychology, the social sciences, design, art, film & theater, therapy, and more professional disciplines.
